Overview

Academic e-Portfolios are digital portfolios that compile students' course-related work, such as essays, posters, photos, videos, and artwork. Academic e-Portfolios can also feature details on a student's extracurricular activities, employment background, volunteer work, and other experiences. Moreover, e-Portfolios are used to document and publicly exhibit student learning. An effective e-Portfolio needs to include more than just a collection of products.
